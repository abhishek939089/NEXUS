    About the program

    The Internet of things describes physical objects with sensors, processing ability, software and other technologies that connect and exchange data with other devices and systems over the Internet or other communications networks. Today demand of IoT is increasing globally and every industry is in need of IoT and its applications. The career demand of IoT as job and start up also booming.

    Perks

    - Learn from basic fundamentals of latest Technologies of IoT - Brief Idea about IOT & use of Embedded Systems in Industries - 100per Practical & Project oriented Course - 100per Live sessions with 1:1 mentorship - Certificate, LOR, Project Certificate will be given to students

    Who can apply?

    Only those candidates can apply who:

    1. are from B.Tech. / B.E.
    2. and specialisation from Engineering
    3. are available for duration of 8 Weeks
    4. have relevant skills and interests

    Terms of Engagement

    Course Completion Certificate from NSDC (Gram Tarang), Internship cum Course Completion Certificate by Skyy Rider, LOR (Letter of Recommendation)
